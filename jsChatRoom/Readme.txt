Way to run the program:
    Turn to the jsChatRoom directory and input "node server.js" in a terminal.
Then open one or more browsers to open the URL:"localhost:8000/login.html".
After that, you could choose to log in or sign up. if the password and 
username are both correct, you could log in to the chatroom. You could log
in with several different accounts at the same time to simulate the scene
that several individuals chat in the same room. You just need to input what
you would like to say and onclick the 'post' button, you will see your message
being sent to the room, meanwhile you could see other people(accounts)' words
in the room.
